# LN3 Basic Proof Methods

<br>

- The major proof techniques include direct proof, proof by contrapositive, proof by contradiction, proof by cases, and proof by induction. We focus on the first four here.

- Before starting a proof, all methods except direct proof should explicitly state "We shall prove by ~".
- For proof by contrapositive, write down the contrapositive statement. For proof by contradiction, write down the negated assumption.
- End the proof with ‚ÄúQ.E.D.‚ÄĚ
