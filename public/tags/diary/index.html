# 《牧羊人》

# River Town: the Metaphor of Chapter VI. ‘Storm’

📅 2024-09-10 | 📝 442 字 | ⏱️ 3 分钟阅读

In Chapter six of River Town, the metaphor of “storm” is incredibly ingenious, much like the line from Border Town: “The heavy rain washed everything, yet it washed away nothing.” True, storms rinsed both HongKong and Fuling the night of HongKong’s handover to China. Yet only when people, like Andy in The Shawshank Redemption, allow the rain to cleanse the blockages and stubbornness deep within their souls, and reach their arms out to the sky, can they attain true liberation.
